Waiting In Line

This project involved creative brainstorming on modifying the experience of a line to help us rapidly prototype paper methods to let us quickly test the usefulness of the ideas.

My idea was to eliminate the main wait of the line: scanning each item individually. Instead, have a RFID on each product that a store is selling, so that customers just have to scan their credit card, walk through a security gate, and their purchase is done.
